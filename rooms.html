Room and Suite Offerings

Pirate's Bay Suite

Do you enjoy Pirates? Then this suite is for you! Themed around our classic pirate-themed rides at WWW this suite brings a fun and relaxing environment for you to wind down and get ready for the next day. This suite includes a pirate ship king-sized bed. As well as a connecting room with pirate-themed bunk beds for your kids. There are also interactive Pirate-themed decorations, games, and activities within each room that are unique to that room! You might even find some hidden treasure!

Includes:

  • Pirate Theme
  • Pirate Souviner
  • King Size Beds
  • Pirate Themed Bunk Beds
  • Pirate Themed Interactive Decor
  • Wild Water Speed Pass
  • Free WIFI
  • Room Service

Light Side Suite

New to our hotel Light Side Suite is an amazing suite geared towards those die-hard Starwars fans out there! Themed around the Jedi council room on Coruscant. These rooms are designed with the power of the force in mind! With included hidden lightsabers in the room and puzzles to open secret doors, you are sure to never be bored staying here! These suites come standard with themed king-sized beds and connecting rooms for the kids with your choice of separate twin-sized beds or bunk beds. Themed decor is also included in these suites as well as direct access to the outdoor pool and bar.

Includes:

  • King size bed
  • Twin size beds
  • Direct access to the pool and bar
  • Lightsaber sovineir
  • Wild Water Speed Pass
  • Free WIFI
  • Room Service

Premium Stay

Premium stay offers the top of the line package for your stay here at WWW. Your choice between a spacious suite or two connecting rooms for you and your family. Included with the premium stay is the in-park Wild Water Speed Pass which allows you to skip the long lines! You also have access to the indoor and outdoor waterparks. These rooms are outfitted with relaxing themes and great views of the lovely Tybee island!

Includes:

  • King and Queen size beds
  • Room Service
  • Twin and Bunk beds
  • Access to the indoor and outdoor waterpark
  • Wild Water Speed Pass

Premier Stay

This is a more reasonable option for those who like to party for a good price. Our premier package offers a spacious bedroom with a king-size bed or two queen-size beds for 2-3 people. This option also comes with a free drink pass for the bar if you are 21 or older. The premier stays package is optimal for those who are trying to party but not break the bank also.

Includes:

  • King Size bed
  • Queen Size bed
  • Drink pass for the bar
  • Free WIFI
  • Room Service
  • Wild Water Speed Pass

Preferred Stay

The preferred stay package offers a great deal for those who are more park-oriented. Within walking distance, to WWW this option puts you in the ideal spot for access to the park! This package also includes a spacious bedroom with two queen size beds and our Wild Water Speed Pass.

Includes:

  • Walking Distance from WWW
  • Spacious Bedroom
  • Queen size beds
  • Free WIFI
  • Wild Water Speed Pass
  • Room Service

Prime Stay

The prime stay package is great for a quick getaway trip. Budget-friendly and to the point this bedroom comes with two queen size beds, as well as our Wild Water Speed Pass! This package also is within walking range of all our amenities within the hotel. Drink pass included.

Includes:

  • Queen size beds
  • Room Service
  • Free WIFI
  • Wild Water Speed Pass
  • Beautiful Views

Value Stay

Our most ideal package for a weekend trip. The value stays package provides you with not only just a place to crash after a long day of water rides but also our drink pass package if you are 21+. This bedroom package offers free wifi, room service, and your choice of a queen-size bed or two full-size beds.

Includes:

  • Drink Pass
  • Queen size bed
  • Twin Size beds
  • Free WIFI
  • Room Service
